About VocTeach

VocTeach aims to develop a web platform that enables vocational teaching practitioners to access and share information about resources, including curriculum-relevant content, and software tools, useful for their blended teaching needs and interests, via the creation of a new site. In the first prototype, we provide a Web platform to support FE teachers by curating and recommending the resources they need to teach English and Maths functional skills.
