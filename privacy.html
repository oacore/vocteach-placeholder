Privacy Notice

Here is an overview about how we process personal data. This page supplements the information provided in The Open University's Privacy Notice.

Registering for our services

When you register for any of our services, we request personal information from you such as your name and email address. We use this information to provide the services you requested and for improving them. We may, with your permission, also contact you about other services and products we think you will be interested in. We retain emails as long as we need to in order to carry out our activities. We will never share your personal information, such as your email address, with any third party except when required for the delivery of our own services or where the law requires us to do so.

Processing your personal data

We collect and store the minimum information necessary for us to carry out our functions and to enable you to participate in VocTeach activities. We keep your information until you tell us to remove it, or until the grant ends, whichever is earlier. We collect anonymous statistics to help us improve our site using Google Analytics. We use this data to understand how our site is used and to make it better. You may also opt out if you wish. See also, a list of all your rights under GDPR.

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy as required by law and as technology changes. We suggest you regularly check this document for the latest changes. We may notify you by email of any changes to this privacy notice.

Any more questions?

If you have any questions regarding our use of your data or for any other reason, please contact us.

VocTeach is delivered by the Open University and Ufi Charitable Trust.
